Design Notes

  • Green tile fireplace: The sage and olive greens in the wallpaper leaves create a beautiful dialogue with your fireplace tile. It's connection without matching.
  • Light vs. the others: This is your brightest, most airy option. The cream ground reflects light. Great if you want the room to feel spacious and cheerful.
  • The hutch question: Paint it warm white to match trim, or sage to be more adventurous. Either works with this palette.
  • Coral's warmth: Coral reads as both pink AND orange. It's inherently warm and will make the room feel inviting.
  • Copper accents: This is the only palette where copper/rose gold makes perfect sense - it echoes the coral flowers.
  • Mood: English cottage garden, romantic, feminine but grounded. Perfect for long leisurely brunches.
  • Pattern vs. color: With a cream ground, the pattern does the heavy lifting. Make sure the scale is large enough for 10' ceilings.
