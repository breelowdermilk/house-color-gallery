Design Notes

  • Green tile fireplace: This palette is designed to complement, not compete. The sage wallpaper relates to the green tile through the green family, but is muted enough to let the tile remain the star.
  • The hutch question: If keeping the hutch, consider painting it the same warm white as trim, or a deeper putty tone. If removing, the damask wall will look stunning uninterrupted.
  • Full-height wallpaper: No chair rail or wainscoting. The damask pattern creates visual interest without needing horizontal breaks.
  • Pattern scale: With 10'+ ceilings, choose a larger damask repeat (24"+) so the pattern reads well from across the room.
  • Mood: This is your most traditional, serene option. It says "elegant dinner party" more than "cozy family meal."
  • Lighting: Warm bulbs (2700K) in a traditional brass or crystal chandelier. Candles on the table.
