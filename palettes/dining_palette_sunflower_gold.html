Design Notes

  • Green tile fireplace: The olive ground of this wallpaper is in the same family as your fireplace tile. They'll relate beautifully without matching exactly. It's harmony, not repetition.
  • The "yellow + something" direction: This palette delivers bold golds paired with soft pinks and deep olive greens. It's fully committed to warmth.
  • The hutch question: An oak hutch would look incredible here. If keeping it, this is the palette that most celebrates wood tones. If removing, the wall pattern is bold enough to stand alone.
  • Oak everything: This palette wants wood. Keep oak trim, oak floors, oak table. It's authentic Arts & Crafts.
  • Pattern scale: Morris florals are traditionally large-scale. With 10'+ ceilings, go big. The room can handle it.
  • Mood: Warm, inviting, slightly bohemian. This room says "linger over dinner." It's cozy but sophisticated.
  • No cool metals: Silver and chrome have no place here. Brass, bronze, copper only.
