Design Notes

  • Green tile fireplace: The olive and forest greens in this palette are in perfect harmony with your fireplace tile. It will feel intentional and cohesive.
  • The boldest option: This is maximalism. The room will be a statement. It's not subtle. Make sure you're ready to commit.
  • The hutch question: Paint it forest green for full drama, or remove entirely. A wood hutch could work but might compete with the pattern.
  • Scale matters: Tropical patterns should be LARGE. With 10'+ ceilings, go for palm fronds that feel life-sized.
  • Colonial vibes: This palette channels British Colonial/plantation style. Dark wood, brass, rattan, and palms create that world.
  • Terracotta vs. Forest: Choose your accent. Both work, but mixing them can get busy. Pick one for chairs/major accents.
  • Mood: Dramatic dinner parties, conversation starter, memorable. This room will feel like an event.
  • Crystal chandelier: Yes. A grand crystal chandelier is perfect here - colonial elegance meets jungle drama.
