Design Notes

  • Green tile fireplace: This is color theory magic - purple and green are complementary colors. The lavender makes the green tile POP. The contrast is intentional and beautiful.
  • The "purple + something" direction: This palette delivers on your stated interest in purple. It's bold but not overwhelming thanks to the soft, muted lavender.
  • The hutch question: Paint it crisp white to match trim, or remove entirely. The wallpaper pattern is the star here.
  • Silver over brass: This palette calls for cool metals. Crystal chandelier is perfect. Save the brass for warmer palettes.
  • Full-height impact: Chinoiserie patterns are traditionally meant to flow floor to ceiling. The botanical motifs will look stunning at full height.
  • Mood: Romantic, feminine, elegant. Perfect for special occasion dinners. Has a slightly English country house feel.
  • The oak question: The oak mantel works beautifully - it provides necessary warmth against the cool lavender. Keep it natural.
