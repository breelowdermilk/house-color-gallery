Design Notes

  • NO white anywhere - all trim, ceiling, and baseboards are colored
  • Wallpaper: Look for Victorian damask patterns in oxblood/wine tones
  • Furniture: Oxblood leather Chesterfield, oak Monkwood-style desk
  • Lighting: Warm amber bulbs (2700K), brass sconces, steampunk desk lamp
  • Curtains: Heavy velvet in deep red or charcoal, floor-to-ceiling
  • Rug: Vintage Persian in deep reds/blues/golds
  • Acoustics: Fabric-wrapped panels in coordinating deep tones
