Design Notes

  • Monochrome envelope: Same teal family on walls, trim, panels, beams creates immersive cocoon effect
  • The fireplace is THE statement: Bold purple pops against teal - avoid diluting with other purple elements elsewhere
  • Coffered ceiling: Light panels + teal beams adds architectural interest without competing
  • Keep the brick: Original red/terracotta fireplace brick complements purple surround
  • Stained glass transoms: Your existing warm-toned glass works beautifully with teal walls
  • Contrast in furniture: Cream, camel, or cognac furniture provides necessary relief from dark walls
  • Persian rug: Traditional rug in reds/golds/teals pulls the whole scheme together
  • Brass everywhere: Antique brass hardware, frames, and fixtures add warmth and period authenticity
